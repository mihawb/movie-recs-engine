Aspiring police officer Ben (Kevin Hart) longs to marry his gorgeous girlfriend Angela (Tika Sumpter), but before he can propose, he must first get the approval of her tough-as-nails brother James (Ice Cube), a top detective in the Atlanta Police Department. When Ben gets accepted into the academy, he finally musters the courage to ask for James' blessing. Still unconvinced that Ben has what it takes to provide for his beloved baby sister, James invites Ben on a ride-along through the toughest parts of town. At first James' efforts to terrify the young trainee go perfectly, but once the pair incur the wrath of Atlanta's most feared criminal, all bets are off..
