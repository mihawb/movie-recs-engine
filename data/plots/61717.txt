Every ninety years, evil returns to try and make it Hell on Earth. Only one born a warrior can fight this evil successfully. Unfortunately for us this time the warrior is more concerned with winning Homecoming Queen than saving the world.......Wendy Wu has everything; popularity, the perfect boyfriend and great best friends. But when a strange monk comes to her and tells her that she is a Yin warrior destined to save the world from a great evil, she doesn't want to hear it. Why not? Because she's too busy campaigning for Homecoming Queen!But when repeat attacks and new and until then, unknown skills show her she really is a Yin Warrior, Wendy realizes that her destiny to save the world from evil is true. Will she fulfill her destiny or go to Homecoming?
