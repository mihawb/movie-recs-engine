Puppy love takes on a new meaning in this animated film featuring a slew of celebrity voices. Kate (Hayden Panettiere, HEROES) is a dominant and driven female wolf, while hairy Humphrey (Justin Long, LIVE FREE OR DIE HARD) lives for the moment. But when the two young wolves are captured by park rangers and taken far away to somewhere big and bad and a real hunter land they bond despite their differences.
