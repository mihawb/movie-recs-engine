Mika, a petty crook, has ended up in jail. There he meets 'Poutine', a crazy and unpredictable inmate. As soon as he has served his sentence, he decides to start a new life. As he prepares to marry a wealthy businessman's daughter, his past catches up with him: 'Poutine' shows up without warning. Mika realizes that he won't get rid of him so soon.
