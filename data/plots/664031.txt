Marta's life sucks. Not only is she an orphan but she's about to be unalived because she's got a terminal illness. Marta's still got her V-card and she wants to lose it to the guy that makes her moist. She enlists the help of her two flamboyant friends and together they go on a madcap romp of an adventure to get the guy of Marta's dreams. Fun family adventure of the year!
