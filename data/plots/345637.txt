Sanjay, a young boy from India is watching Super Team, a cartoon. As the Super heroes break out of a cocoon, Sanjay is distracted by a ringing sound. His father is on the other side of the room meditating with a bell, and Sanjay gets annoyed. After Sanjay turns up the T.V's volume, the Father turns it down and then off with his remote control. Then he signals for Sanjay to sit next to him and join in. After handing him the figurine of the lead character, Sanjay sits next to his father and closes his eyes. The young boy starts to day dream about Hindu gods and goes on an epic and colorful adventure. Hindu gods become powerful super heroes in his day dream. After his dream, Sanjay's father allows him to turn the T.V. back on. To his father's surprise, Sanjay has his a new Super Team! After drawing the gods that he saw (instaed of turning on the T.V.), he shows it to his father and the two bond because of it!
