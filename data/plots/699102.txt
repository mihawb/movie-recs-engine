A young man finds himself at the fork in the and has to make a life changing decision. Whether or not to keep living ratchet on the streets of Harlem or the chance to reconnect with everyone he ever loved. He feels a presence that leads him back to his roots. In the small town of joneston the boy finds himself back at his church and surrounded by old friends and family. The life ahead is a clear blue sky for him with nothing but happiness, a peace of mind and a true love.
