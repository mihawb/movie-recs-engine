Project Arrowhead, a military experiment gone wrong has opened an inter-dimensional rift, setting loose unthinkable creatures in the town of Maine. After a storm rips through Maine, local Hollywood artist David Drayton, (Thomas Jane), his son Billy and their next door neighbor drive into town for supplies, Whilst at the supermarket, shoppers are alerted by the presence of military convoys, emergency vehicles & the towns emergency siren. Shortly after the town is engulfed by a thick mist, it becomes apparent that this is no ordinary fog. Barricaded in the supermarket for days on end, frightened shoppers fight for survival against the horrific creatures that lurk outside, unaware that perhaps the biggest monster is already among them.
