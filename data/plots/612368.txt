In late 1970s France, twenty-five-year-old Pierre returned from Wyoming to take over his father's farm, determined to usher in a new era of modern agriculture and progress. After almost two decades, a loving wife and two adolescent children, business is flourishing, and happiness blesses Pierre's household. However, despite painful sacrifices and back-breaking work to keep the farm running, fast-growing debts start piling up, threatening everything. Now, there is no turning back.
