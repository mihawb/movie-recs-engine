Looking for a way out of a lifetime of crime, sweet, kind, and decent petty criminal Farès, the son of scheming con woman Danny, has developed a plan to legitimately control the distribution of the 'Mr Freeze' ice lollies in North Africa. And then, Farès discovers his crooked, cruel mother has gambled away his savings. Now, the dream of moving into a villa in sunny Spain with Lamya, the object of his desire, starts to fade away unless Farès agrees to pull off one last job for Scarface wannabe Poutine, the new local drug boss. However, has Farès what it takes to carry off the drug deal and make his dream come true?—Nick Riganas
