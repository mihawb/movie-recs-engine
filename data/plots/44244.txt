Mitchie can't wait to return to Camp Rock so that she and love-interest Shane can spend the summer making music and having fun with their friends and bandmates. But when a rival camp, Camp Star, run by famous music producer Axel Turner, opens across the lake, many Camp Rock campers and counselors ditch Camp Rock and join Camp Star. Mitchie becomes too busy running the camp and preparing for the "Camp Wars" to have much fun, but Shane and friends help her open up and look on the bright side of summer.
